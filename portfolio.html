Uppertunities is a mobile app that will help you locate nearby career development experiences that give you access to different internships and volunteer work in the area you live in. It will help you create an account that allows you to put your educational and work history information. It will allow you to build a resume within your account and give you in-app browsing to autofill your built in resume when applying for jobs within the app. It will give you, the customer, a support chat box for any questions
